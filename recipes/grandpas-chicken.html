Grandpa's Chicken

Home

Description

Look, we all remember you could always count on grandpa for two things... falling asleep at the dinner table and cooking some DAMN GOOD CHICKEN

So today my friends, we'll explore the latter. Buckle up because when you're done with this recipe you're going to have made a chicken so good you just might find a wife

Ingredients

  • A big old bird (chicken)
  • A tall glass of your favorite beverage
  • Every gosh darn seasoning in your kitchen
  • A catiously optimistic disposition

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450 F
  2. Change your mind and turn in down to 425 F
  3. Lather up that chicken with every seasoning under the sun
  4. Throw it in the oven and open up a good book
